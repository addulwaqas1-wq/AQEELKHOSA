Concrete Cutting Services

Advanced Solutions for Reinforced Concrete

Reliable Concrete Cutting in UAE

Fajr Al Dhaid offers comprehensive concrete cutting services tailored to meet the demands of modern construction projects in Sharjah and the UAE. Whether you need to resize a concrete structure, create openings, or remove sections entirely, our skilled team is equipped to handle it.

Our Concrete Cutting Techniques

We employ a variety of cutting methods to ensure efficiency and precision:

  • Wall Sawing: Ideal for cutting precise openings in vertical concrete surfaces for doors, windows, and ventilation ducts.
  • Floor Sawing: Used for cutting horizontal flat surfaces such as roads, runways, and concrete slabs.
  • Wire Sawing: Perfect for cutting thick or complex concrete structures where traditional saws cannot reach.

Applications

Our services are widely used for:

  • Creating openings for elevators and staircases.
  • Modifying existing structures for renovation.
  • Expansion joint cutting.
  • Demolition of specific building sections.
