Floor Sawing Services

Efficient Cutting for Roads and Slabs

Professional Floor Sawing in UAE

Fajr Al Dhaid provides top-tier floor sawing services (also known as slab sawing) for horizontal flat surfaces. We utilize powerful diesel and electric floor saws capable of cutting through reinforced concrete and asphalt with speed and precision.

Key Services

  • Road Cutting: Cutting asphalt and concrete for road repairs and utility installation.
  • Expansion Joints: Creating expansion joints in new concrete slabs to prevent cracking.
  • Trenching: Cutting trenches for laying pipes and cables.
  • Slab Removal: Cutting slabs into manageable sections for removal.

Equipment & Safety

Our fleet includes high-performance floor saws that ensure deep cutting capabilities. We prioritize safety and efficiency, ensuring that all cuts are clean and precise to facilitate the next stages of your construction project.
