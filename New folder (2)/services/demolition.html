Demolition Services

Safe and Controlled Structural Demolition

Expert Demolition Works in UAE

Fajr Al Dhaid offers comprehensive demolition services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects in Sharjah and across the UAE. We specialize in controlled demolition techniques that ensure safety and efficiency while minimizing environmental impact.

Our Demolition Services Include

  • Selective Demolition: Removing specific parts of a building while preserving the remaining structure.
  • Total Demolition: Complete removal of buildings and structures to clear the site for new construction.
  • Interior Strip-out: Removing non-structural elements like walls, ceilings, and flooring for renovation.
  • Concrete Breaking: Breaking and removal of concrete slabs, foundations, and retaining walls.

Safety First

Demolition is a high-risk activity that requires careful planning and execution. Our team adheres to strict safety protocols and local regulations to ensure the safety of our workers, the public, and adjacent properties. We also handle debris removal and site clearance.
