Professional Core Cutting in UAE

Fajr Al Dhaid is a premier provider of core cutting services in Sharjah and across the UAE. Our team utilizes advanced diamond core drilling machinery to create precise circular openings in reinforced concrete, brick, and masonry structures.

What is Core Cutting?

Core cutting, or core drilling, is the process of drilling clean, circular holes in concrete. This technique is essential for plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and telecommunication installations where pipes or cables need to pass through walls, floors, or ceilings.

Our Core Cutting Capabilities

  • Diameter Range: We can drill holes ranging from 10mm to 1000mm in diameter.
  • Depth: Our equipment can drill to significant depths as required by your project specifications.
  • Angle Drilling: We can perform stitch drilling and angle drilling for complex requirements.
  • Clean & Dust-Free: We use wet drilling techniques to minimize dust and vibration, ensuring structural integrity is maintained.
