Wall Sawing Services

Precise Openings in Vertical Structures

Expert Wall Sawing in UAE

Fajr Al Dhaid specializes in wall sawing services, providing precise cutting solutions for vertical concrete surfaces. Our advanced track-mounted saw systems allow for accurate cuts in reinforced concrete walls of varying thicknesses.

Applications of Wall Sawing

  • Door and Window Openings: Cutting precise openings in existing walls for new doors and windows.
  • Ventilation Ducts: Creating pathways for HVAC systems.
  • Structural Modifications: Removing sections of walls for building alterations.
  • Staircase Openings: Cutting openings for new staircases or elevators.

Benefits of Our Service

We ensure minimal vibration and noise during the cutting process, preserving the structural integrity of the surrounding area. Our experienced operators deliver clean, straight cuts with no over-cutting, reducing the need for patching.
